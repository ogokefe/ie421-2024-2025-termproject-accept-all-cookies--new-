Project Overview

Analysis of Summer Olympics data to understand patterns in Olympic success and athletic performance.

Research Questions

  1. How does the number of athletes a country sends relate to its medal count?
  2. Does diversity in sports participation impact a country's medal tally?
  3. How does athlete performance vary with age and experience in different Olympic sports?

Key Visualizations

Key Findings

Athletes Count vs Medal Count

  • Strong positive correlation (R² = 0.86)
  • Random Forest model showed best performance
  • GDP per capita significantly impacts medal conversion

Sports Diversity Impact

  • Optimal number of sports: 12-15
  • Concentration Index threshold: 0.3
  • R² Score: 0.82 for Random Forest model

Age-Performance Analysis

  • Swimming: Peak at 20-24 years (R² = 0.76)
  • Gymnastics: Peak at 16-20 years (R² = 0.81)
  • Athletics: Peak at 24-30 years (R² = 0.73)

Data sourced from Olympedia.org and IOC databases
